Visit Quad Cities

We're full of real fun and real experiences, and we're proud to call this place home

There are many fun activities, festivals and shopping for all ages!

>

Putnam Museum

The Putnam Museum is a place where people connect with history and ideas. The Figge Art Museum is the premier art exhibition and education facility.

>

Farmers Market

The Freight House Farmers Market is a year long market located on the banks of the Mississippi River. It features over 200+ farmers, bakers, makers, and atrisinal food producers.

>

QC River Bandits

Quad Cities River Bandits are a Minor League Baseball team. Home games are played at Modern Woodman Park, Formerly John O’ Donnel Stadium built in 1931.

>

Village of East Davenport

Village of East Davenport is a Historic Mississippi river neighborhood with shopping, art, cafes and special street and park events. For a special treat, Visit Largomarcino’s.
